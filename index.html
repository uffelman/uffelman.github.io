About

A decade of craft.

I specialize in building polished, performant applications for iOS and macOS. Over the past decade, I've shipped products used by millions of people at companies like Square and Level.

My work has been featured in Apple Stores — from the Level smart lock to the Weebly app for iPad, which Apple selected to demo the iPad Pro at its launch.

As a former Silicon Valley attorney and startup consultant, I advised companies through billion dollar acquisitions and IPOs. I hold a B.A. cum laude from Vanderbilt University and a J.D. cum laude from the University of Michigan Law School.
